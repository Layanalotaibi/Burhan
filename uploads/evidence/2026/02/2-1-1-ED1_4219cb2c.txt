───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. PURPOSE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This policy establishes cybersecurity requirements for the management 
of all information and technology assets within the organization to 
ensure confidentiality, integrity and availability.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
2. SCOPE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

This policy applies to all information and technology assets owned 
or operated by the organization, including but not limited to:
- Infrastructure (servers, storage systems)
- Applications and software services
- Network equipment
- Workstations and end-user devices
- Peripherals (printers, scanners)

[ERROR: Missing Operating Systems from scope]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3. ASSET CLASSIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

All assets must be classified based on criticality and the data they 
include or process. 

Classification levels:
- Critical: Essential for business operations, contains highly 
  sensitive data
- High: Important for operations, contains sensitive data
- Medium: Standard business assets
- Low: Minimal impact assets

4. LIFECYCLE REQUIREMENTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Requirements must be defined for the following stages:
- Acquisition and procurement
- Processing and operation
- Storage and archival
- Disposal and destruction
